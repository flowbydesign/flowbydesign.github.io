Macau / Sept.14

_One of two special administrative regions of the People's Republic of China, the other being Hong Kong. A former Portuguese colony, Macau was administered by Portugal from the mid-16th century until late 1999, when it was the last remaining European colony in Asia. Macau is one of the world's richest cities, with the highest GDP per capita by purchasing power parity as of 2013.

Hong Kong / Sept.10

_One of two special administrative regions of the People's Republic of China. It is renowned for its expansive skyline and deep natural harbour. Hong Kong is one of the most densely populated areas in the world.

Shanghai / Sept.10

_The most populous city in China. Once a fishing and textiles town, Shanghai grew to importance in the 19th century. A global city, Shanghai exerts influence over global commerce, finance, culture, art, fashion, research and entertainment.

Beijing / Dec.09

_An integral part of China’s history for centuries. Beijing is renowned for its opulent palaces, temples, and huge stone walls and gates.

Sichuan / Dec.09

_On May 12, 2008 Sichuan was hit by a magnitude of 7.9/8.0 earthquake northwest of the provincial capital of Chengdu.
